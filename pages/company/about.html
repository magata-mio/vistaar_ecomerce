Our Infrastructure

End-to-End Value Chain Management:

Wildcraft has a strong R&D team led by domain experts and a global network of vendors. Wildcraft employs 2500 staffs across 2,00,000+ sq. ft. manufacturing facilities in North & South India with a capacity to produce 10 mn+ pieces per annum.

Multi-Channel Distribution:

Wildcraft is equipped with an extended geo footprint of 200+ exclusive brand stores across 60+ cities with 5000+ retail points-of-sale across India. This also includes over 1000 points-of-saleacross departmental stores & hypermarkets.Wildcraft also has a strategic partnership with Flipkart & Myntra.
